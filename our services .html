Our Services - Graphic Design Program

At Creative Media Academy, we provide an intensive graphic design course that covers essential skills in the industry. In this program, you will learn everything from the basics of Photoshop to advanced design techniques that will help you succeed in the creative industry.

Week One: Introduction to the Program

  • 1. Introduction to Graphic Design: You will get a solid foundation of the principles of design, color theory, and composition.
  • 2.Photoshop Basics: Learn the tools and techniques used to create digital art, starting with creating a composite image from multiple elements.
  • 3. Typography Poster Design: Explore the use of typography in design and create a poster using different fonts, sizes, and placements for a strong visual impact.

Week Two: Logo Design & Magazine Creation

  • 1. Logo Design: Learn the fundamentals of logo design, including creating logos for businesses or clients. Understand branding and how to design a logo that communicates the essence of a brand.
  • 2. Magazine Layout Design: Master the skills of creating professional magazine layouts, understanding balance, grid systems, and how to present content visually appealing.

Week Three: Advanced Tools and Creation

  • 1. Introduction to InDesign: Familiarize yourself with InDesign, the industry-standard software for creating professional layouts for print and digital media.
  • 2. E-book Creation: You will learn to design and format an e-book, focusing on layout, fonts, and visual design principles.

Week Four: Design Projects

  • 1. Custom Brochure Design: Learn how to design a custom brochure, understanding the importance of layout, content organization, and the visual hierarchy.
  • 2. Business Card Design: Design a custom business card that reflects the branding and professionalism of a business or client.

week five: final touches
